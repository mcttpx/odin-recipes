Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

Craving a delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich? Look no further, for you have arrived at the place to make these dreams a reality.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry Jelly

Steps

  1. First, toast your slices of bread
  2. Then spread peanut butter on both sides of the sandwich, this is crucial
  3. Then, place a healthy dollop of strawberry into the middle and form the sandwich
  4. This method keeps your bread safe from being made soggy by the jelly, and provides a much better ratio of PB to J.
